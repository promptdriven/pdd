## Shot Details

### The Mold
- Material: Steel or aluminum (metallic, machined)
- Surface: Polished where material contacts, textured elsewhere
- Channels: Visible runner system and gates
- Temperature: Hot (could show heat shimmer)

### The Flow
- Color: Amber/orange when molten, cooling to solid color
- Movement: Viscous flow, not water-like
- Behavior: Spreads until it hits walls, then stops
- Filling: Complete coverage of cavity

### Key Visual Moments

1. **The Constraint:** Material hits a wall and stops - this is THE key visual
2. **The Precision:** Mold walls are exact, sharp, intentional
3. **The Fill:** Material takes the exact shape of the cavity

## Technical Accuracy

Real injection molding details:
- Material enters through sprue/runner system
- Fills under pressure
- Air vents allow gas escape
- Cooling happens quickly
