## Scene Description

The grandmother's weathered hands carefully darning the wool sock. Needle and thread weave through the fabric in a steady, practiced rhythm. A cross-stitch pattern forms over the hole in the heel. Thread pulled taut with each deliberate pass.

The hole gradually closes with neat, even stitches. The final stitch is completed. Small silver scissors enter the frame and snip the thread cleanly. The grandmother holds up the repaired sock, examining her careful work with quiet satisfaction.
