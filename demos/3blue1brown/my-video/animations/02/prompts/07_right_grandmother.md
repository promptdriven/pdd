## Character

**The Grandmother** - consistent across all segments:
- 60-70 years old
- Silver-gray hair, neat appearance
- Patient, practiced expression
- 1950s period-appropriate clothing

---

## Scene Description

1950s grandmother sitting in a cozy living room by a lamp, carefully darning a wool sock with a needle and thread. Warm incandescent lighting creates soft shadows. Period-appropriate clothing - perhaps a cardigan or house dress.

She's completing the final stitches on a sock repair, her movements careful and precise. The practiced ease of someone who has done this thousands of times. Patient satisfaction in skilled handwork.

The overall tone should feel nostalgic/historical - viewing a skilled domestic craft from another era. This will have sepia grading applied in post to match the developer side.
